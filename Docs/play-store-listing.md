## Full Description (max 4,000 characters)

```
EthioStat is your personal finance and telecom assistant, built for Ethiopia.

It automatically tracks your Ethio Telecom assets — voice minutes, SMS bundles, and data/internet packages — by reading SMS from 994. For financial transactions, you choose which banks to track: add Telebirr, CBE, Awash, Dashen, or any of 25+ Ethiopian banks in Settings, and the app reads their SMS to track every transaction. No typing. No internet. Everything stays on your phone.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHY EthioStat?

Ethio Telecom (994) sends you SMS every time your packages change — EthioStat reads those automatically. Your banks and Telebirr also send SMS for every transaction — add them in Settings, and EthioStat organizes everything into a clear, beautiful dashboard.

✓ Works with ALL major Ethiopian banks
✓ 100% offline — your data never leaves your phone
✓ Supports English, Amharic (አማርኛ), and Afaan Oromo
✓ Ethiopian calendar built in

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FINANCIAL TRACKING

• Automatic SMS parsing — income and expenses detected instantly
• Transaction history with smart categories (purchases, transfers, loans, fees)
• Export transaction reports to CSV — share or save for your records
• Net balance, total income, and total expense summaries
• Per-bank dashboards — see each bank's activity separately
• 90-day historical scan — imports 3 months of transactions when you first install
• Party name extraction — see who sent you money or who you paid

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TELECOM ASSET MANAGEMENT

• Track your Ethio Telecom airtime balance
• Monitor data packages (internet, voice minutes, SMS bundles)
• See package expiry dates and usage percentages
• Bonus fund tracking
• USSD balance sync — check your balance right from the app

SUPPORTED BANKS & SERVICES

EthioStat is 100% offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your financial information stays on your device — always. No account required. No sign-up. No internet needed.

• No cloud storage

HOW IT WORKS

1. Install EthioStat and grant SMS permission
2. Add your banks and Telebirr in Settings — you choose which sources to track

4. New transactions from your chosen sources are detected in the background
5. Open the app anytime to see your complete financial picture
```
